## 6. CONTRARIAN TAKES

-   **Mainstream View**: The goal of AI is Artificial General Intelligence (AGI), achieved by scaling models.
-   **Contrarian View**: "Nobody wants intelligence, period. I want something else, right? And that's something else is always specific." - Omar Khutab (20260116_The_a16z_Show_How_Foundation_Models_Evolved_). We need Artificial Programmable Intelligence (API) – systems built around models to solve specific problems, not just bigger models.
-   **How to Bet on It**: Focus on building AI *systems* for narrow, high-value problem domains. Use frameworks like DSPy to formally specify intent and integrate LLMs into modular workflows, rather than relying on generic prompts or waiting for AGI.

-   **Mainstream View**: Diversify your business portfolio; "hold co's" are a smart way to grow.
-   **Contrarian View**: "Hold co's are overrated... Most rich guys I know are focused on one big business and built it for a long time." - Nick Huber (20260115_My_First_Million_When_everything_works_). Spreading yourself across multiple ventures often means you don't master any, leading to diluted effort and subpar results.
-   **How to Bet on It**: Pick one core product or business idea and commit to it fully. Resist the urge to launch multiple micro-products simultaneously. Go deep on a single problem and market.

-   **Mainstream View**: AI is universally good and will improve quality of life for everyone.
-   **Contrarian View**: "AI is bullshit and unsustainable and will decrease quality of life for the 99%." - Nick Huber (20260115_My_First_Million_When_everything_works_). The massive energy demands of AI data centers will drive up energy costs for ordinary consumers, while the business benefits might primarily accrue to a small percentage of companies.
-   **How to Bet on It**: Be mindful of the energy footprint and cost of your AI solutions. Look for efficiencies in model usage. Consider niche problems where AI provides outsized, undeniable value that justifies its operational cost, rather than chasing every "AI-powered" trend.
