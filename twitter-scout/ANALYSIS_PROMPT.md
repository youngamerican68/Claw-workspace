## Focus Areas
- AI agents, OpenClaw/Clawdbot ecosystem
- Claude Code, vibe coding
- Business opportunities, pain points
- Tools people wish existed
- "I'd pay for X" signals
- Major announcements in AI/agents space

## Analysis Guidelines
- Be direct and opinionated
- Include real numbers (views, likes, followers)
- Connect opportunities to Paul's stack (OpenClaw, landing pages)
- Prioritize actionable over interesting
- Skip the hype - call it out when you see it
- Quality over quantity
